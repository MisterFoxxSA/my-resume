Animations

Spirited Away

Cast: 20 July 2001
Released: Rumi Hiiragi, Miyu Irino, Mari Natsuki

"Spirited Away" is a mesmerizing animated film directed by Hayao Miyazaki. It follows the enchanting journey of a young girl named Chihiro who stumbles upon a magical world ruled by spirits, witches, and gods. As she navigates this strange realm, she must summon her courage to rescue her parents and return to the human world.

Howl's Moving Castle

Cast: Chieko Baisho, Takuya Kimura, Akihiro Miwa
Released: 20 November 2004

"Howl's Moving Castle," released on 20 November 2004, is a whimsical fantasy film directed by Hayao Miyazaki. The story centers around Sophie, a young woman transformed into an old lady by a witch's curse, who finds herself aboard the magical, walking castle of the mysterious wizard Howl. With voices from Chieko Baisho, Takuya Kimura, and Akihiro Miwa, the film is a visually stunning adventure filled with magic and romance.

Comedy

Forest Gump

Cast: Tom Hanks, Robin Wright, Gary Sinise
Released: 6 July 1994

"Forrest Gump," released on 6 July 1994, is a heartwarming comedy-drama featuring Tom Hanks as the lovable and simple-minded Forrest Gump. The film follows Forrest's extraordinary life journey through historical events, always maintaining his endearing innocence and optimism, with memorable performances by Robin Wright and Gary Sinise.

Snatch

Cast: Brad Pitt, Jason Statham, Dennis Farina
Released: 23 August 2000

"Snatch," released on 23 August 2000, is a fast-paced crime comedy directed by Guy Ritchie. With an ensemble cast including Brad Pitt, Jason Statham, and Dennis Farina, the film intertwines multiple storylines of diamond theft, underground boxing, and quirky characters in London's gritty underworld.

Liar Liar

Cast: Jim Carrey, Maura Tierney, Jennifer Tilly
Released: 18 March 1997

"Liar Liar," released on 18 March 1997, is a hilarious comedy starring Jim Carrey as Fletcher Reede, a fast-talking lawyer who is cursed to tell the truth for 24 hours. As he navigates the chaos that ensues from his inability to lie, Fletcher's journey becomes both a comedic and heartfelt exploration of honesty and personal growth.

Survival

Cast Away

Cast: Tom Hanks, Helen Hunt, Nick Searcy
Released: 22 December 2000

"Cast Away," released on 22 December 2000, features Tom Hanks in a gripping survival drama directed by Robert Zemeckis. The film tells the story of Chuck Noland, a FedEx executive stranded on a deserted island after a plane crash. His struggle for survival and journey of self-discovery forms the heart of this compelling narrative, with Helen Hunt and Nick Searcy in supporting roles.

The Road

Cast: Viggo Mortensen, Kodi Smit-McPhee, Robert Duvall
Released: 3 September 2009

"The Road," released on 3 September 2009, is a haunting post-apocalyptic survival film based on Cormac McCarthy's novel. Starring Viggo Mortensen and Kodi Smit-McPhee, the film follows a father and son's harrowing journey through a desolate landscape, facing starvation and danger while clinging to hope and each other.

The Martian

Cast: Matt Damon, Jessica Chastain, Kristen Wiig
Released: 11 September 2015

"The Martian," released on 11 September 2015, is a thrilling science fiction survival film directed by Ridley Scott. Starring Matt Damon as astronaut Mark Watney, the film chronicles his struggle to survive alone on Mars after being presumed dead by his crew. With a captivating mix of ingenuity and resilience, Watney's journey is a testament to human spirit and determination.

Randoms

Released: 8 July 2010

"Inception," released on 8 July 2010, is a mind-bending thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. Starring Leonardo DiCaprio, the film delves into the world of dreams and corporate espionage, where skilled thieves steal secrets from within the subconscious. With an ensemble cast including Ken Watanabe and Joseph Gordon-Levitt, "Inception" is a visually stunning and intellectually stimulating cinematic experience.

Pulp Fiction

Cast: John Travolta, Samuel L. Jackson, Uma Thurman

"Pulp Fiction," released on 21 May 1994, is an iconic film directed by Quentin Tarantino. Featuring an all-star cast including John Travolta, Samuel L. Jackson, and Uma Thurman, the movie weaves together multiple interconnected stories of crime and redemption in Los Angeles, known for its sharp dialogue, dark humor, and unforgettable scenes.

Fight Club

Cast: Brad Pitt, Edward Norton, Helena Bonham Carter
Released: 10 September 1999

"Fight Club," released in 1999 and directed by David Fincher, stars Brad Pitt and Edward Norton in a dark and provocative exploration of identity, consumerism, and rebellion. The film follows an unnamed narrator who forms an underground fight club with the enigmatic Tyler Durden, leading to a gripping and mind-bending journey into the human psyche.
